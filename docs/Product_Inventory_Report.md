# Product & Inventory Analysis Report

## Objective

Analyse product inventory and sales performance to support better inventory management.

## Analysis Performed

- Product summary
- Inventory valuation
- Top & bottom inventory products
- Fast & slow moving products
- ABC classification
- Inventory health assessment

## Key Findings

- Identified high-value inventory items.
- Classified products into A, B, and C categories.
- Detected understocked and overstocked products.
- Identified fast-moving and slow-moving products.

## Recommendations

- Monitor Class A products closely.
- Replenish understocked items promptly.
- Reduce excess stock for overstocked products.
