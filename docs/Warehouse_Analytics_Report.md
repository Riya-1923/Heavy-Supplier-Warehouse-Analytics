# Warehouse Analytics Report

## Objective

Evaluate warehouse performance, utilization, and inventory distribution to identify operational improvements.

## Analysis Performed

- Warehouse stock summary
- Warehouse utilization analysis
- Capacity analysis
- Stock movement analysis
- Product category distribution
- Operational bottleneck detection

## Key Findings

- Identified warehouse utilization levels.
- Measured stock movement across warehouses.
- Detected products below reorder and safety stock levels.
- Highlighted operational bottlenecks.

## Recommendations

- Replenish products below reorder level.
- Optimize warehouse capacity utilization.
- Reduce excess inventory.
- Improve inventory balancing between warehouses.
